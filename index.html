GOYAL & CO.
A trusted name in the real estate industry, Goyal & Co. has reinvented the real estate landscape in Ahmedabad since 1971. Spread across 3 of the fastest growing cities, Ahmedabad, Bangalore and Mumbai, the group fosters transparency, quality, communities and experience in the most dignified manner and delivers each project as promised. The group has 200 successful projects, innovation in properties and ever-growing legacy.

They needed an agency that could uphold the sanctity of the brand yet make it relevant to a diverse group of people digitally. Thus, the communication was spread across digital media platforms while creating a digital AV commercial and branding a one-of-a-kind affordable housing project.
ASTRAL ADHESIVES
A nationally renowned brand in the pipes and adhesives industry, Astral Pipes have been pioneers in the piping industry, whilst their sister brand, Astral Adhesives have strived to better the current adhesive offerings in the market. The brand has been proactive in marketing and advertising with many celebrity associations like those with Salman Khan and Varun Dhawan.

The magnitude of the brand demands the need for an army of channel partners. To effectively communicate to the channel partners, consumers and the media, a customised communication plan was put in place that had a different tonality, approach and creative.
